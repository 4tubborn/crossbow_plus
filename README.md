# Crossbow Plus
A Minecraft datapack that modifies the logic of crossbows.
* supported versions: 1.21-1.21.10
* develop in 25w43a

The part of updating bundle contents needs modifying in 1.21.11 due to the change of item modifier.

Actually I am not sure about the min compatible version. Maybe you can have a try :)

## Features
### More Projectiles
* All blocks and items can be charged into crossbows!
### More Special Projectiles
* Tnt, shulker bullets, wind charges, fireballs, end pearls, potions, spawn eggs and more! They can be shot as special entities, bringing a great deal of fun!
### Bundles
* Now you can charge projectiles from a bundle!
### Compatible
* Projectiles are compatible with inifinity, multishot enchantments!
